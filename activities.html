Resistance Movement in peripheral regions of São Paulo

Anhanguera, Luta & Resistência - ALR

The objectives of the group is to act as a resistance entity, given the atual situation of Brasil, where education was trown apart, where individualities are no more respected; where black people are being killed and disrimined.

Currently, the group has a project to recover the memories of Morro Doce, a peripheral region of the city of São Paulo. How this neighborhood originated past years ago and how it evolved until today. Interviews are being made with people livin there, and in sequence a magazie will be written to show to everyone the results. The main issue in this is that there is a "collective memeory" in the neighborhood saying that it originated from some specific situation, but in fact, that is not completelly wright. So, that question is investigated in the project.
